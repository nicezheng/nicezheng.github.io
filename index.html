About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate at the School of Artificial Intelligence, Beijing University of Posts and Telecommunications (BUPT), working under the mentorship of Prof. Wei Wang and co-supervised by Prof. Yi Wang. My research interests lie at AI4Science and social computing. My work mainly focuses on the application of AI for natural science, particularly in dune and ocean fields. I also working on mining and modeling signaling of GitHub to promote online collaborative work.
